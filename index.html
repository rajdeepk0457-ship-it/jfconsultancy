JF Consultancy (JFinder – B & S Consultancy) is a specialized recruitment and staffing firm focused on fulfilling customer‑specific hiring requirements across PAN India. We bring deep industry knowledge and a results‑driven approach to deliver cost‑effective and high‑quality recruitment solutions.

Our team is dedicated to delivering pre‑screened, job‑ready, and quality candidates who align with both technical and cultural requirements. We aim to be a trusted recruitment partner for organizations seeking fast, reliable, and scalable IT & Non‑IT hiring support.

About Us

JFinder is an India‑based job search and recruitment platform specializing in both IT and Non‑IT job opportunities. Unlike traditional job boards, JFinder aggregates and sources thousands of career opportunities from multiple platforms across India—bringing them together in one centralized search experience.

We maintain a strong and diverse talent pool of over 5,000+ skilled professionals, enabling us to meet hiring demands across various industries and experience levels. Our recruitment process focuses on understanding client needs, role expectations, and delivery timelines to ensure effective placements.

With a commitment to quality, transparency, and speed, JFinder continues to support organizations and job seekers by bridging the gap between the right talent and the right opportunity.
